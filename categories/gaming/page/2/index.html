  • Dead Cells

    Dead Cells in a few words is the story of a headless prisoner trying to figure out why he still exists. It’s a fast paced game that has quite a bit of speed and the tight controls, a controller is definitely a must for full enjoyment. The game can be played through in about thirty or so minutes and that’s part of what makes it so awesome. Every playthrough is just a little bit different, from the weapons you get or the paths you decide to take.…
