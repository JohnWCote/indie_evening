  • Lovers in a Dangerous Space Time

    2020-05-12

    This game is great fun as it provides an opportunity for cooperation as oppose to competition with it’s co-op modes. I’m basing it off of a 2 player playthrough, but it now supports up to 4 players. The overall premise of the game is to pilot a spaceship to save little trapped critters from mechanical monsters. Everything is whismical in nature and the game rewards helping others. There’s some level of strategy in having to man shields, guns and the motor of the ship with just two people.…
